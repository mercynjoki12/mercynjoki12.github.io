Welcome!

Just like home Bahari Beach Hotel is a homely spacious resort offering the standards and services of a first class hotel. Its location, the good-natured staff and the informal atmospheres make it a great place to stay.Facing the renowned Nyali Beach on Kenya’s North Coast, overlooking the warm azure waters of the Indian Ocean, Bahari is set within lush and colourful gardens, where palms, tropical flowering shrubs and bougainvillea form a superb backdrop for the water garden and its soothing streams and fountains.
